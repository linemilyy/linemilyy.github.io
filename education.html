Ed.gov

Overview

The current US Department of Education website provides an opulent source of information for users to browse. Although the website is a good source of information, users may be overwhelmed by the abundance of text on each page. To minimize the time wasted on trying to locate desired information, my team and I proposed to reorganize and recategorize the existing pages, simplifying streamlining the information provided on each page.

Our project goal was as follows:

Redesign the US Department of Education website to provide a user-friendly navigation system that offered ease of use to users of all backgrounds, optimizing the time spent on the website by minimizing time wasted scrolling through nonessential information.
